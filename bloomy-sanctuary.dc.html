Bloomy Sanctuary
3D Media Art / 01 : 17 / Planning, Look Dev, Simulation
d'strict Korea Inc.
for Media Art Platform LED.ART
2023-12 / 2024-02
Creative Direction : d'strict Korea Inc.
Planning : Hyunyoung Kim
Look Development : Hyunyoung Kim, Jaehoon Sung
Simulation : Hyunyoung Kim
Compositing : Hyunyoung Kim
This work explores a calm and tranquil atmosphere through soft vegetation, drifting flower petals, and subtle movement of light. Designed for physical LED display environments, it creates a quiet and immersive spatial experience. The work was later featured on Insydium's official Instagram account.
부드러운 식생과 은은한 빛의 움직임을 통해 고요하고 차분한 공간감을 표현하였습니다. 꽃잎이 천천히 흩날리는 장면과 따뜻한 조명을 활용하여 실제 LED 공간에서도 자연스럽고 편안한 분위기를 연출하고자 하였습니다. 본 작품은 Insydium 공식 Instagram 계정에 소개되었습니다.

The 32nd Seoul Music Awards
3D Motion Graphics / 00 : 51 / 3D Design, Animation
Client : Seoul Music Awards
Production : Studio WA
2022-12 / 2023-01
Direction : William Sean Kim (Sehun Kim)
3D Design : Hyunyoung Kim
Animation : Hyunyoung Kim
Sound : No Place on Earth (Marco Martini)
Contributed to the VCR visuals for the 32nd Seoul Music Awards, including the title sequence and bumper videos. Inspired by kinetic forms combining wood and gold, the visuals were designed to capture the vibrant atmosphere of the K-pop awards ceremony.
제32회 2023 서울가요대상의 타이틀 시퀀스와 범퍼 영상을 포함한 VCR 영상 제작에 참여하였습니다. 나무와 골드 소재를 활용한 키네틱 비주얼을 통해 K-POP 시상식의 화려한 분위기를 표현하였습니다.

Blooming : Cherry Blossom
3D Media Art / 01 : 06 / Planning, Look Dev, Simulation
d'strict Korea Inc.
for Media Art Platform LED.ART
2023-06 / 2023-11
Resolution. 6K
Creative Direction : d'strict Korea Inc.
Planning : Hyunyoung Kim
Look Development : Hyunyoung Kim
Simulation : Hyunyoung Kim
Compositing : Hyunyoung Kim
Centered around a horizontally extended cherry blossom tree, this work creates a contemporary spatial composition inspired by the atmosphere of spring. The concept was developed through AI-assisted ideation and was presented on The Hyundai Seoul media wall during its 3rd Anniversary event in 2024.
가로로 길게 뻗은 벚꽃나무를 중심으로 봄의 분위기와 현대적인 공간감을 표현하였습니다. AI 기반의 초기 콘셉트 리서치를 바탕으로 레이아웃을 발전시켰으며, 2024년 더현대서울 3주년 기념행사에서 더현대서울 미디어월에 상영되었습니다.
